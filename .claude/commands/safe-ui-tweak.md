# Safe UI Tweak

Apply a UI-only improvement with low regression risk.

## Allowed Files
- index.html
- style.css

## Forbidden
- Do not change decision logic in app.js.
- Do not remove or weaken legal disclaimer messaging.

## Required Output
1. What was changed in UI.
2. Why this improves clarity or usability.
3. Mobile + desktop impact.
4. Quick rollback note.
